The 1% Problem

Marcus's Current Process (1% response)

  1. Manually write email → 15 mins
  2. Send to 1 prospect
  3. Wait (rarely hear back)
  4. Chase manually if time allows
  5. Result: 1 conversation per 100 emails = 25 hours per lead

AI System (5-15% response)

  1. AI researches 100 prospects overnight
  2. Writes 100 personalized emails (in Marcus's voice)
  3. Sends all 100 in minutes
  4. Auto-follows up 3x over 2 weeks
  5. Auto-responds to inquiries
  6. Only alerts Marcus when lead is HOT
  7. Result: 5-15 conversations per 100 emails

Marcus's Time:

Before: 25 hours grinding for 1 lead

After: 30 mins closing 5-15 warm leads

"No Human Can Replicate You"

The AI doesn't replace Marcus. It AMPLIFIES him.

Trained on Marcus's exact language:

  • "We can make it happen"
  • "Custom bespoke solutions to suit your needs"
  • "We're not just selling products, we're solving your problems"

Sends from HIS email/LinkedIn. Uses HIS expertise. Works while he's in the workshop. Scales Marcus's voice to 100s of prospects simultaneously.

Our AI Technology / 006

This Isn't ChatGPT.
This Is Enterprise Lead Generation.

Our system is a complex, custom-built AI infrastructure — not a standard LLM (Large Language Model). While ChatGPT is a general-purpose conversation tool, we've engineered a specialist system purpose-built for your business.

  • Built by real developers — Custom Python/Node.js backend, not a prompt-engineered chatbot
  • Trained specifically on your voice — Marcus's communication style, your industry expertise, your deal flow
  • Multi-channel orchestration — Manages email, SMS, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp simultaneously across multiple campaigns
  • Lead intelligence layer — Real-time company research, intent signals, and automatic lead scoring
  • 24/7 tireless outreach — Never sleeps, never takes holidays, scales beyond human capacity
  • Conversation memory — Remembers every interaction, maintains context across weeks or months of dialogue

Why This Matters for TM Tarpaulins

  • You're not paying for compute time or API calls — you're investing in a system that compounds in effectiveness as it learns your market
  • It handles the volume (100+ prospects per week) that would require hiring 2-3 BDMs
  • You stay in control — it sounds like Marcus, acts like your company culture, maintains the trust relationships you've built
