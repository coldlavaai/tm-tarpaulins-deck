Partnership Model / 009

Partnership
Model

"The prices in this presentation are our standard rates. You earn commission on every client you bring in."

How It Works

  • You source the clients — we deliver the work
  • Commission paid on setup fees for every project you bring in
  • Commission paid on monthly recurring revenue — passive income that grows with every client
  • Cold Lava handles all build, management, and support behind the scenes
  • For variable/bespoke work — you send us a brief, we quote, you present to the client

  • Happy to work through you, or deal with clients directly — your call
  • Everything can be white-labelled under your brand if preferred
  • Your clients, your relationships — we're the engine behind the scenes
